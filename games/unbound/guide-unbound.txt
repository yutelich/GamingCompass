  1. INFO DASAR & STARTER
================================================================

Pokemon Unbound adalah ROM hack GBA berbasis Pokemon Fire Red
buatan Skeli. Region: Borrius. Gen 1-8. 800+ Pokemon.
Versi terbaru: v2.1.1.1.

PILIH STARTER:
--------------
[GIBLE] Dragon/Ground -> Garchomp
  - TERBAIK OVERALL. Speed + Attack brutal, coverage luas.
  - Mega Stone (Garchompite): Shadow Base 1F
  - Cocok untuk player yang suka agresif.

[BELDUM] Steel/Psychic -> Metagross
  - Tank solid, tahan banyak type.
  - Mega Stone: Shadow Base 1F (butuh Level 2 Keycard)
    ATAU reward Mission #050: Portal Purge
  - CATATAN di mode Insane: lokasi Mega Stone berbeda
    (Crystal Peak, reward dari Marlon)
  - Evolusi lambat: Beldum Lv20 -> Metang, Lv45 -> Metagross

[LARVITAR] Rock/Ground -> Tyranitar
  - PALING SUSAH di awal! 4x lemah Grass, tapi Gym 1 Grass.
  - Mega Stone (Tyranitarite): Shadow Base 1F
  - Hanya untuk yang suka challenge.

================================================================
  2. DIFFICULTY MODE
================================================================

VANILLA/CASUAL : Santai, untuk pemula
DIFFICULT      : Trainer pakai held items, moveset lebih baik
EXPERT         : AI cerdas, EV/IV dioptimasi, gym lebih brutal
INSANE         : No items in battle, level scaling ketat,
                 field effects tambahan. SANGAT UNFAIR.

!! JANGAN langsung pilih Insane kalau baru pertama main !!

LEVEL SCALING:
  - Gym leader/boss: menyesuaikan ke Pokemon TERTINGGI di party
  - Random trainer: menyesuaikan ke Pokemon TERENDAH di party
  -> Jaga party supaya tidak terlalu jomplang levelnya!

================================================================
  3. FITUR QoL PENTING
================================================================

HM SYSTEM BARU  : Tidak perlu HM slave! Pokemon apapun bisa
                  pakai HM di overworld.
DEXNAV          : Chain encounter Pokemon spesifik - bagus
                  untuk cari shiny atau farming EV.
EXPERIENCE SHARE: Semua Pokemon di party dapat EXP otomatis.
KARAKTER KUSTOM : 300+ kombinasi skin, rambut, outfit.
DAILY EVENTS    : Butuh Real Time Clock (RTC) aktif di emulator.
PHYS/SPEC SPLIT : Sistem Gen 4+ - moves lebih masuk akal.
MAX RAIDS       : Ada mode raid seperti Sword/Shield.

================================================================
  4. GYM GUIDE (8 GYM + GIMMICK COUNTER)
================================================================

LEVEL CAP: G1:20 -> G2:26 -> G3:32 -> G4:36 ->
           G5:45 -> G6:52 -> G7:58 -> G8:64

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 1 | MIRSKLE | Dresco Town | Tipe: Grass | Level Cap: 20
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: FOG - akurasi semua Pokemon turun (termasuk punyamu)
TEAM: Roselia, Tangela, Ludicolo, MEGA Sceptile

COUNTER:
  - Pakai move yang tidak kena efek akurasi:
    Swift, Aerial Ace, atau move akurasi 100% yang kuat
  - Fire type adalah counter terbaik
  - Selesaikan battle cepat - Leftovers + Grassy Terrain
    bikin musuh susah di-KO kalau kamu main lambat
  - Punya Larvitar? Siapkan Fire type backup dari luar!

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 2 | VEGA | Crater Town | Tipe: Dark | Level Cap: 26
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: DAMAGE OVER TIME - Pokemon non-Dark kamu kena
         damage setiap akhir turn
TEAM: Liepard, Honchkrow, Spiritomb, Pawniard, MEGA Absol

COUNTER:
  - Main AGRESIF - setiap turn yang dibuang = HP terkuras
  - Fighting type sangat efektif vs sebagian besar timnya
    (kecuali Spiritomb - pakai Fairy)
  - Kalau punya Dark type sendiri, jadikan frontliner
    (tidak kena efek damage)
  - Bawa banyak healing items, jaga HP di atas 50%

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 3 | ALICE | Blizzard City | Tipe: Flying | Level Cap: 32
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: TAILWIND - semua Flying musuh 2x lebih cepat,
         damage ke Flying dikurangi
TEAM: Skarmory, Dodrio, MEGA Pinsir, Gliscor, Crobat

COUNTER:
  - Rock dan Electric type adalah pilihan utama
  - Ice type untuk Gliscor (Ground/Flying)
  - Karena Tailwind, hampir pasti musuh lebih dulu serang
  - Punya Trick Room? Setup di awal untuk balik speed advantage

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 4 | MEL | Fallshore City | Tipe: Normal | Level Cap: 36
------------------------------------------------------------
!!!! GYM YANG PALING SERING BIKIN PLAYER BINGUNG !!!!

GIMMICK: INVERSE BATTLE - SEMUA type matchup DIBALIK!
TEAM: Porygon-Z, Swellow, MEGA Kangaskhan, Type:Null, Snorlax

!!! JANGAN BAWA FIGHTING TYPE !!!
Di Inverse Battle, Normal jadi KEBAL Fighting.
Fighting malah jadi lemah. Banyak player mati konyol disini.

COUNTER:
  - Pakai GHOST-TYPE MOVES dari Pokemon non-Ghost
    (Normal yang biasanya imun Ghost, di sini jadi lemah Ghost)
  - Electric, Ice, Rock, Steel, Dark type juga efektif
  - Cara mudah berpikir: bayangkan type chart dibalik total
  - Porygon-Z adalah ancaman pertama - prioritaskan KO ini
    pakai Ghost move atau Steel type

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 5 | GALAVAN | Dehara City | Tipe: Elec/Steel | Lvl: 45
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: LEVITATE - SEMUA Pokemon musuh imun Ground!
         Plus ini adalah DOUBLE BATTLE!
TEAM: Magnezone, Togedemaru, Klinklang, MEGA Steelix, Emolga

COUNTER:
  - TRICK: Pakai move GRAVITY untuk cancel efek Levitate!
    Dengan Gravity aktif, Ground moves kembali normal.
  - Fire type sangat efektif vs Steel (Camerupt/Mega Camerupt)
  - Grass dan Fighting type juga bisa
  - Di Double Battle: setup dulu, prioritaskan KO Emolga
    (lemah Rock dan Ice)

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 6 | BIG MO | Antisis City | Tipe: Fighting | Lvl: 52
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: BAN - Flying, Fairy, dan Psychic DILARANG masuk!
TEAM: Kommo-o, Pangoro, Bewear, MEGA Heracross, Toxicroak,
      Lucario

COUNTER:
  - Poison type: efektif vs Kommo-o (Dragon/Fighting)
    Crobat, Tentacruel, atau Gengar adalah pilihan bagus
  - Ghost type tidak bisa kena Fighting moves normal
  - Rencanakan team sebelum masuk! Kalau team kamu
    full Flying/Fairy/Psychic, bakal masuk slot kosong

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 7 | TESSY | Polder Town | Tipe: Poison | Level Cap: 58
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: TYPING CHANGE - Pokemon kamu berubah tipe sesuai
         2 move pertama yang dipakai dalam battle!
TEAM: Toxapex, Roserade, Salazzle, MEGA Venusaur,
      Dragalge, Nihilego

COUNTER:
  - Pilih 2 move pertama dengan hati-hati untuk dapat
    typing yang bagus. Contoh: Steel + Fire = coverage luas
  - Ground type efektif vs Poison: Earthquake masih aman
    dipakai sebagai move ke-2 setelah setup move
  - Kalau bingung, pakai Normal type move dulu baru strong move

------------------------------------------------------------
GYM 8 | BENJAMIN | Redwood Village | Tipe: Bug | Lvl: 64
------------------------------------------------------------
GIMMICK: DEVOLUTION - Pokemon musuh revert ke pre-evo
         saat kalah, lalu lanjut fight dengan 2 "nyawa"!
TEAM: Leavanny, Scolipede, MEGA Scizor, Araquanid,
      Volcarona, Lurantis

COUNTER:
  - Fire dan Flying type: counter utama Bug
  - Volcarona SANGAT BERBAHAYA (Fire/Bug) - KO sebelum
    dia sempat setup Quiver Dance
  - Manfaatkan devolution: setelah revert ke pre-evo
    biasanya jauh lebih lemah, bagus untuk healing/setup
  - CATATAN: Mega Scizor tetap Mega setelah devolution!

POST-GYM REMATCH:
  - Setelah jadi Champion, rematch semua 8 gym leader
    di Battleground (Seaport City)
  - 4 leader tersedia per hari dengan team lebih kuat
  - Menang = dapat Z-Crystal tipe mereka
  - Leaders Gauntlet: kalahkan semua 8 berturut-turut!

================================================================
  6. UNLOCK MEGA EVOLUTION
================================================================

!! Tanpa selesaikan Tarmigan Mansion, Pokemon tidak bisa
   Mega Evolve meskipun sudah punya Mega Stone + Key Stone !!

CARA KE TARMIGAN MANSION:
  Route 11 -> Cliff Cave (pakai Strength) ->
  Upper Route 11 -> jalan ke utara -> Tarmigan Town -> Mansion

LANGKAH UNLOCK:
  1. Masuk Tarmigan Mansion (4 lantai + basement)
  2. Cari 3 secret switch tersembunyi di dalam (ada di statue)
  3. Kalahkan Successor Maxima (setara gym leader)
  4. Reward: MEGA KEYSTONE -> Mega Evolution aktif!

MEGA STONE STARTER:
  Gible   -> Garchompite  : Shadow Base 1F
  Beldum  -> Metagrossite : Shadow Base 1F (Level 2 Keycard)
                            ATAU Mission #050: Portal Purge
  Larvitar-> Tyranitarite : Shadow Base 1F

  [MODE INSANE]: Metagrossite ada di Crystal Peak
                 (reward dari Marlon)

MEGA STONE LAIN:
  Cameruptite  : Cinder Volcano (mantap vs Gym 5 Galavan)
  Mega Rayquaza: Ajarkan Dragon Descent dari NPC dekat
                 Battle Frontier entrance

================================================================
  7. MISSION GUIDE & MILESTONE
================================================================

TOTAL MISI: 84 | Tidak ada yang permanently missable!

MILESTONE KRITIS:
  45 Misi Selesai -> Unlock LATIAS / LATIOS
  65 Misi Selesai -> SHINY CHARM

MISI PALING PENTING:
  #050 Portal Purge    -> Metagrossite (non-Beldum starter)
  The Endless Nightmare-> Member's Card -> catch DARKRAI
                          (tidur di Tarmigan Inn malam hari)
  Nine Tails of Snow   -> Alolan Vulpix Egg
  Island Survivor      -> Pokemon langka di pulau terpencil

TIPS FARMING MISI:
  - Buka Mission Log rutin setelah masuk kota baru
  - Ajak bicara SEMUA NPC di setiap kota
  - Setelah punya Fly/Surf, revisit kota lama
  - Cloud Burst mini-game: practice sampai hafal pola

================================================================
  8. LEGENDARY (26 TOTAL)
================================================================

LATIAS / LATIOS   : Selesaikan 45 misi
DARKRAI           : Mission "The Endless Nightmare" ->
                    Member's Card -> tidur Tarmigan Inn malam
JIRACHI           : Route 13, post-game, syarat tertentu
TORNADUS/THUNDURUS: Roaming di overworld post-game
KYUREM            : Ruins of the Void (Underwater)
SHAYMIN           : Flower Paradise
RAYQUAZA          : Crystal Peak (post-game)
MEGA RAYQUAZA     : Ajarkan Dragon Descent dari NPC
                    dekat Battle Frontier entrance
SWORDS OF JUSTICE :
  Cobalion  -> Magnolia Fields
  Terrakion -> Victory Road
  Virizion  -> butuh selesaikan misi tertentu

TIPS CATCH LEGENDARY:
  - False Swipe + Thunder Wave: HP 1 + paralisis = easiest catch
  - Bawa Ultra Ball / Dusk Ball (malam atau di gua)
  - Bawa banyak Revive dan Full Restore
  - Kalau KO legendary, hampir semua bisa di-encounter ulang
    setelah masuk/keluar area atau save-reload
  - Simpan Master Ball untuk roaming legendary (Tornadus dll)

================================================================
  9. TRAINING & EV GUIDE
================================================================

EXP GRINDING TERBAIK:
  HP Trainer  : Full party Audino, level scaling otomatis
                = EXP per battle tertinggi. Ada dari awal game!
  Elite 4     : Post-game, level tinggi + farming uang sekaligus
  Battle Frontier: High level trainers, beragam type

EV TRAINING:
  Icicle Cave Secret Area : Hanya Alolan Sandshrew (Defense EV)
                            Unlock via misi
  DexNav Chaining         : Chain Pokemon yang kasih EV spesifik
  Battle Frontier Black   :
    Belt NPC              : Bayar BP -> max semua EV instant!
                            (Post-game, paling efisien)
  Vitamin                 : Protein/Iron/dll - cepat tapi mahal
                            Pakai Infinite Money cheat untuk skip

NATURE & BREEDING:
  Everstone   : Pokemon holding ini saat breeding -> pass down
                nature-nya ke keturunan
  Hidden Power: NPC Hidden Power Changer di Battle Frontier
                (malam hari) bisa ubah IVs untuk HP type tertentu
  Hidden Ability: Female 80% chance pass down ability slot
  Nature NPC  : Di Tehl Town ada NPC yang bisa ubah nature
                (bayar dengan item tertentu)

================================================================
  10. POST-GAME
================================================================

BATTLE FRONTIER:
  Battle Tower      : Farm BP untuk TM, items, EV training
  Black Belt NPC    : Max semua EV dengan BP (terbaik!)
  Hidden Power NPC  : Ubah IVs Pokemon (malam hari)
  Gym Leader Rematch: 4 leader per hari, Z-Crystal reward
  Leaders Gauntlet  : Beat 8 gym leader berturut-turut

NEW GAME+ (NG+):
  - Unlock setelah beat main story
  - Carry-over progress tertentu
  - NPC Battle Frontier (Black Belt EV trainer) muncul
    di Crater Town dari awal

CHECKLIST POST-GAME:
  [ ] Catch 26 Legendary/Mythical
  [ ] Dapat Shiny Charm (65 misi)
  [ ] Complete Pokedex (467 Pokemon di routes)
  [ ] Max EV semua Pokemon (Battle Frontier Black Belt)
  [ ] Beat Leaders Gauntlet (8 gym leader berturut-turut)
  [ ] Catch Darkrai (Mission Endless Nightmare)
  [ ] Mining di KBT Expressway (fossil Pokemon)
